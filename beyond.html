TEASER

Infinity, the infinitely large, the infinitely pretty, the infinitely cloudy. According to the Robert, a cloud is by definition a mass of water vapor condensed into fine droplets held in suspension in the atmosphere. A cloud is therefore difficult to represent. It is not defined. Infinity is also an overflowing imagination. Just like the vision that we got about clouds, let’s try the impossible! This show gives you a unique experience by plunging you into the immensity of cosmic clouds. We decide to do a reproduction of these planetary nebulae in order to allow everybody to assist at this rare phenomenon.
